Integration Toolkit

Practical methods to raise your VC

The CARE Protocol (Crisis Response)

Use when VC drops below 20 or in acute distress:

C = Check Your Body

  • Am I safe right now? (Physical safety first)
  • When did I last eat/drink/sleep?
  • Take 5 deep breaths

A = Acknowledge What's Real

  • "I'm in crisis. This is temporary."
  • "My VC is very low right now."
  • "I need support."

R = Reach Out

  • 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
  • Trusted friend/family
  • Therapist/crisis line
  • Do NOT isolate

E = Engage Basics

  • Eat something
  • Drink water
  • Lie down if you can't sleep
  • Stay where you're safe

Daily Integration Practices

1. Emotionally Honest Journaling (10-20 min/day)

Purpose: Lower G₁ by processing suppressed emotions

How:

  • Stream of consciousness, no editing
  • Write how you actually feel, not how you "should" feel
  • No one else will read this - be completely honest
  • Focus on emotions, not just events

Prompts:

  • "What am I actually feeling right now?"
  • "What am I avoiding feeling?"
  • "What wave is trying to crash that I'm blocking?"

2. AI-Assisted Processing

Purpose: Get reflection without judgment

Tools: ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI chat

How:

  • Journal first (raw, unedited)
  • Then share with AI: "I just wrote this. Help me see patterns."
  • AI can spot suppression, avoidance, resistance you miss
  • No shame, no judgment - just pattern recognition

3. Body Scan Meditation (5-10 min/day)

Purpose: Reconnect awareness to body sensations

How:

  • Lie down or sit comfortably
  • Start at toes, move up to head
  • Notice sensations without judging
  • Emotions live in body - you're learning to feel them again

4. Weekly VC Tracking

Purpose: Measure progress objectively

How:

  • Every week, rate your G₁, R, A (0-10 scale)
  • Calculate VC
  • Track in spreadsheet or journal
  • Notice patterns: What raises/lowers your VC?

30-Day Formula Verification Challenge

Test If VICS Is Real

The Protocol:

Every Morning (5 minutes):

  1. Rate suppression (G₁): 0-10 scale
  2. Rate resistance (R): 0-10 scale
  3. Rate avoidance (A): 0-10 scale
  4. Calculate VC using formula
  5. Predict: "Today will be [easy/medium/hard] based on my VC"

Every Evening (5 minutes):

  1. Rate actual day difficulty: 0-10 scale
  2. Track: Emotional incidents, conflicts, functionality
  3. Compare: Did low VC predict hard day?
  4. Compare: Did high VC predict easier day?

After 30 Days:

  1. Plot VC scores vs. day difficulty ratings
  2. Calculate correlation
  3. If correlation > 0.7: Formula is real
  4. If correlation < 0.3: Formula is bullshit

This is your proof. The equation either works or it doesn't. Track 30 days. You'll know.

When To Seek Professional Help

Therapy Recommended When:

  • VC consistently below 30
  • Suicidal thoughts present
  • Unable to function at work/relationships
  • Substance use increasing
  • Self-harm urges/actions
  • Dissociation frequent
