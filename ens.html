How To Use ENS

Step 1: Feel The Signal

Don't analyze yet. Just notice: What am I feeling in my body right now?

Step 2: Identify The Color

Green? Yellow? Red? Yield? (Most feelings map to one)

Step 3: Take Signal-Appropriate Action

  • Green ‚Üí Continue
  • Yellow ‚Üí Pause, assess
  • Red ‚Üí Stop, create distance
  • Yield ‚Üí Pause everything, integrate

Step 4: Trust The Signal

Your nervous system knows before your mind does. Trust the body.
