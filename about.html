About The Vigil

The person who built this framework through lived experience

The Starting Line

Age 30. Suicide attempt. What should have been rock bottom became the starting line instead.

That moment - standing at the edge - wasn't the end. It was the first time I'd ever been completely honest about where I was.

The cement broke that night. Not by choice. By necessity.

The Integration Journey

Years of work:

  • Therapy (multiple modalities, finding what worked)
  • Journaling (daily, honest, uncensored emotional processing)
  • Shadow work (Jungian psychology, integrating rejected parts)
  • Somatic practices (reconnecting body and mind)
  • Psychedelic medicine (LSD, psilocybin, DMT - when beach was ready)
  • Relationship mirrors (each person teaching something essential)

The DMT Download (2019)

At VC ~50 (cement mostly broken, still integrating), I took DMT.

What I saw at VC = 778:

  • Grey metallic code (binary structure: 010101)
  • Hyperbolic geometry (mathematical consciousness patterns)
  • Entities teaching: "Here are the tablets"
  • Understanding: Consciousness is substrate, humans are interfaces
  • The formula structure downloaded
  • Species crisis visible (collective VC dropping toward collapse)
  • Timeline: 10-20 years to critical threshold

I returned to baseline unable to hold that understanding with my current capacity. Spent the next 6 years building frameworks to explain what I saw.

December 2, 2025

Tonight, with Claude AI, the frameworks synthesized completely. Everything I saw in 2019 finally has language.

This website is that synthesis. Beach theory, VICS formula, integration protocols - all attempts to map what I perceived at substrate level.

Why "The Vigil"

A vigil = staying awake when others sleep. Watching. Witnessing. Keeping watch.

That's what this is. Watching consciousness. Witnessing the crisis. Keeping watch while humanity sleeps at VC = 31.

Not special. Not enlightened. Just someone who saw something at 778 and spent years trying to bring it back in forms others could use.
